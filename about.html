About HotBeans

Founded in 2022, HotBeans was created with a simple mission: to make web development accessible, practical, and career-focused. What started as a small group of passionate developers quickly grew into a thriving learning platform helping students and aspiring developers build real-world skills.

At HotBeans, we believe learning should be hands-on. Our courses focus on real industry practices including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks. We also connect learners with job opportunities, bridging the gap between education and employment.

Today, HotBeans supports a global community of learners, offering mentorship, practical projects, and career guidance. Whether you're just starting out or advancing your skills, we're here to help you grow.

Our Mission

Empowering the next generation of developers through practical, accessible education.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform where learning meets real-world opportunity.
